01 / Problem Statement

Why Garbage Classification?

Manual waste sorting is slow, expensive, and inconsistent. As global waste increases, humans cannot keep up with the volume, leading to massive amounts of recyclable material ending up in landfills.

Research Question

Can a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) accurately classify garbage images into 10 distinct categories to power real-time, automated sorting systems?
