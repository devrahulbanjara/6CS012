03 / Group Insights

Patterns Across All Four Runs

Transfer Learning Wins

Pretrained backbones like VGG16, ResNet50, and MobileNetV2 landed at 92 to 95% test accuracy. CNNs made from scratch made upto 65 to 80%.

SGD Underperforms Adam

For each of us, Deeper CNN with SGD lost roughly 5 to 15% test accuracy compared to Adam. Adam just converged faster on this dataset. Exception was Rahul's.

Ablation Confirms Regularization

Taking out dropout or batch norm caused the biggest gap between train and test. Regularization really matters on a dataset of this size.

Embeddings Help, Mostly

GloVe and Word2Vec lifted test F1 for three of the four of us on sarcasm detection by cutting overfitting. One run already peaked with plain LSTM.
